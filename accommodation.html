Hotels/Accommodation

Augusta offers hotels in the downtown area within walking distance of the conference venue. The Marriott is the recommended hotel within a 10 minute walking distance of the venue, with the Hyatt as the next best option. Holiday Inn is recommended for value and is either a short ride or slightly longer walk from the conference.
