Privacy Policy

We are committed to preserving your privacy. Please read the following privacy policy to understand how we use and protect information.

By using this website you consent to the use of data under the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Disclosure of your Data

Any data you provide to us will be treated as confidential. Unless required to do so by law, we will not share, sell or distribute any of the data you provide to us without your consent.

How we use Cookies

A cookie is a small text file that is passed between our server and your web browser. We use this information to track your movements through our site, to find out how it is being used and to assess its usefulness. Personal data about you is not collected during the tracking process.

This web site uses cookies to collect web site usage information via local analytics software (Piwik). This site also uses social networking features provided by AddThis.com - their privacy policy outlines their use of your data (we do not collect any personal data via the AddThis social networking tools).

What type of information is collected?

  • The data collected relates to each specific visit to the web site. The information gathered includes the duration of the visit, the path taken between pages, the type of computer that is being used and the approximate location of the visitor.

  • Our analytics tools do not collect any personal information such as user name or email address.

How is this data used?

  • Data collected by us is used to develop and improve the quality of this web site. Some of this data is collected by the web server (the computer that holds the web site) and some is collected using cookies that are sent to the web browser of each web site visitor.

Managing Cookies

  • You can choose not to accept cookies and still use this web site, but some functionality will be lost. For example, user preferences will not be retained if cookies are disabled.

  • The documentation for your web browser (usually accessible from the ‘Help’ menu) will provide you with instructions on how to manage cookies with further information available at AllAboutCookies.

Third Party Websites

Because we want your experience of our site to be as informative and useful as possible, we provide a number of links to websites operated by third parties. Please be aware that we do not control such websites and that such websites may send their own cookies to users, or otherwise collect data or solicit personal information. We assume no responsibility for the information gathering practices of websites that you are able to access through our site, and we encourage you to review each website's privacy policy before disclosing any personally identifiable information.

Security

We employ security measures to protect your data from access by unauthorised persons and against unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction or damage.
