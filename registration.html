Registration Fees

All delegates will be entitled to admission to the conference on each of the days and will receive a printed copy of the conference proceedings (provided post-event). Registration to the conference includes participation with coffee and lunch and also includes a social event which will include dinner. Authors are required to register by 6th June 2025.

Each accepted paper must be accompanied by at least one registration. Multiple papers from the same author(s) are subject each paper having a unique registration fee being paid.
